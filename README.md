# SoapsHungerStamina

**Make stamina and hunger work together in survival gameplay.**

SoapsHungerStamina bridges MMOCore stamina and vanilla survival. Sprint, jump, and swim drain MMOCore stamina. When stamina runs out, hunger drains instead. It's that simple.

## What It Does

- Sprinting, jumping, and swimming cost MMOCore stamina
- Once stamina hits zero, overflow costs are paid in hunger
- Shows real-time stamina on action bar or boss bar
- Players feel stamina management in combat, travel, and exploration
